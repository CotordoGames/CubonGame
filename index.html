the story...

In CUBON, you play as Cubon. His arcade was blown up by a mysterious figure who crawled up from underground. On the verge of bankruptcy, Cubon discovers a secret passage and drags his girlfriend Cubette down with him, hungry for revenge.

He soon learns the truth: his arcade was a target of the underground dictator, King Cubit. So begins Cubon's journey to free everyone from the evil king...

the speed

walking / jumping

ARROWS + Z

y'know. the basics. you've done this before.

shiftpound

SHIFT (air)

slam toward the ground at high speed. bounce back up higher than a normal jump.

dash

X

launch forward and destroy any enemies in your path.

wall slide / jump

WALL + Z

hug a wall and you'll slide rather than fall. press Z to wall jump.
