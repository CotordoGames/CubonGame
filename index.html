the story...

In CUBON, you play as, well, cubon. Cubon's arcade was blown up by someone who came up from his basement

in the beggining of the story, setting off the main events. after the attack and rebuilding of his arcade,

nobody returned due to fear of another attack. So cubon, on the verge of bankruptcy and tired, goes down

and discovers where the person had come from: a secret passage. he ends up dragging his girfriend,

cubette, down there for revenge. he later finds out that his arcade was a target by the underground

dictator, king cubit. the reason his arcade was blown up was becuase his subjects could escape his underground

kingdom through it. And so, cubon's journey to free everyone from the evil king begins...

the speed

CUBON has several special moves that you can use to get araound!

walking/jumping

c'mon... do i really have to explain what we do everyday?

shiftpound

by pressing shift while in the air, cubon propels toward the ground at high speeds! if you keep holding shift,

cubon will bounce back up, higher than he can regulary jump! can also be used to dismantle enemies

dash

by pressing x, cubon will launch forward, destroying any enemies in his path! in the future, cubon will be able

to destroy blocks with it

wall slide/jump

when "hugging" a wall, cubon will slide down rather than fall. if you press z while this is happening, you will

walljump!
