the story...

In CUBON, you play as, well, cubon. Cubon's arcade was blown up by someonw who came up from his basement

in the beggining of the story, setting off the main events. after the attack and rebuilding of his arcade,

nobody returned due to fear of another attack. So cubon, on the verge of bankruptcy and tired, goes down

and discovers where the person had come from: a secret passage. he ends of dragging his girfriend,

cubette, down there for revenge. he later finds out that his arcade was a taret by the underground

dictator, king cubit. the reason his arcade was blown up was becuase his subjects could escape through it.

And so, cubon's journey to free everyone from the evil king begins...
